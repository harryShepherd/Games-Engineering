Cube Zone

The Player

You play as the player, a red cube in the Cube Zone world. Your goal is to stay aware of your surroundings, defeat your enemies, and progress through the randomly selected levels laid out before you.

Cube Zone is a simple 2-dimensional platformer wherein you, the player, move through levels killing enemies and progressing through harder and harder challenges.

The Enemy

In your journey you will encounter enemies that are looking to stop you. You can defeat them by shooting them - but make sure you do it before they shoot you. Plan your path, enemies can come from anywhere.
